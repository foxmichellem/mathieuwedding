Our story

Brian and Katie met while working together at Yorkshire Manor. After 2 years, Brian finally worked up the courage to ask Katie on a date. Although she didn't say yes at first (the jury is still out on if she was truly 'busy"), Katie agreed to go to dinner with the cute guy in the Detroit Lion's scrubs. And she is sure glad she did! The two fell hard and fast. They began building their lives in Grand Rapids. Katie graduated from Grand Valley and began her nursing career, while Brian began his career at AT&T. About a year and a half after they began dating, Brian pulled off an amazing proposal in San Francisco with the help of close friends and family. And now here we are!
